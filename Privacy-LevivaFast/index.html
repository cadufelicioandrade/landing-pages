Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Leviva Fast application ("the App", "we") collects, uses and protects user information. By installing or using Leviva Fast, you agree to the terms described here.

1. Who we are

Leviva Fast is developed and maintained by Kodaru Systems, an independent software developer. If you have any questions about privacy, contact us at cadufelicioandrade@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

DataWhen it is collectedPurpose
Email address and name (Google) If you choose to link your Google Account Identify your account and sync data across devices
Fasting records, fasting protocol, start and end times, weight, gender, app settings Normal use of the app Track your fasting windows and keep your history (locally and, if signed in, in the cloud)
Advertising identifier and ad interaction data When ads are displayed (Google AdMob) Serve and measure the performance of ads
Crash and technical performance data (crash logs) When an error occurs in the app Identify and fix bugs (Firebase Crashlytics)
Usage data and app events Normal use of the app Understand which features are used most (Firebase Analytics)
Subscription status and purchase history When you subscribe to the Premium plan Unlock and validate access to premium features while the subscription is active

The weight, gender and fasting protocol you provide are used solely to track your fasting windows and show your progress, and do not constitute medical advice. Leviva Fast is not a medical application and is not a substitute for guidance from healthcare professionals.

3. Where your data is stored

Most of your data (fasting records, protocols, reminders and settings) is stored locally on your device. If you choose to link your Google Account, that same data is also synced in encrypted form to Google Firebase servers (Firestore and Authentication), so that you can restore it on another device.

4. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We use the following third-party services, each subject to its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics) — Google's privacy policy
  • Google AdMob — ads displayed in the free version of the app — Google's advertising policy
  • Google Play Billing — processing of monthly and annual subscriptions (we have no access to your payment card details)

5. Ads and Privacy Sandbox

Leviva Fast displays ads provided by Google AdMob in the free version. These ads may use device identifiers for personalization, according to your Android privacy settings (including the Privacy Sandbox/Topics API, where available). You can limit ad personalization in your Android device's privacy settings.

Users with an active monthly or annual Premium subscription no longer see ads in the app.

6. Your rights

At any time, you may:

  • Use Leviva Fast fully offline, without linking a Google Account;
  • Unlink your Google Account in the app's Settings;
  • Request deletion of the data stored in the cloud by writing to cadufelicioandrade@gmail.com;
  • Manage or cancel your subscription at any time in the Google Play Store, under Payments & subscriptions;
  • Uninstall the app, which removes all data stored locally on the device.

7. Children

Leviva Fast is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to cadufelicioandrade@gmail.com.
