Data safety & privacy

Delete your DropPulse account and data

This page explains how to request deletion of your DropPulse account and what happens to your data afterward. DropPulse (com.app.beberagua) is developed and operated by Kodaru System — Carlos Eduardo Felicio de Andrade.

How to request deletion

01

Delete your account inside the DropPulse app

The fastest option, available directly in the app:

  1. Open DropPulse on your device
  2. Tap the Me tab in the bottom navigation bar
  3. Scroll to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted
02

Or request deletion by email

Write to cadufelicioandrade@gmail.com with the subject "Account deletion request — DropPulse", including the email address you used to sign in to DropPulse. That address is how Kodaru System identifies which account to delete.

03

Confirmation and timeline

Kodaru System will confirm receipt of your request by email. Your DropPulse account and personal data are permanently deleted within 30 days of that confirmation. You do not need an active account or a login to submit a request through this page.

What happens to your data

Permanently deleted

  • Account profile (name, email, and photo linked to your sign-in)
  • Hydration history and drinking statistics
  • Preferences (weight, gender, language, unit of measure)
  • Data synced to the cloud (Firestore)
  • Account authentication record

Retained when required

  • Aggregated, anonymous usage statistics that cannot identify you
  • Advertising identifiers already processed by partners (Google AdMob), unlinked from your account
  • Transaction and subscription records required by applicable tax law, kept separately from your identity
Retention period: once your request is confirmed, personal data associated with your DropPulse account is deleted within 30 days. Encrypted backup copies may take up to 90 days to be fully purged from Kodaru System's systems.
