FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does it work on every website?+

Yes. Office Automation works on any website with HTML tables — including dynamic pages rendered by JavaScript. If you can see a table on screen, the extension can extract it.

Is my data safe? Does it leave my computer?+

Your table data never leaves your browser. The extraction and export happens entirely on your machine. We only communicate with our servers for authentication and license validation — never your data.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?+

Absolutely. Cancel from your account dashboard in one click. You keep Pro access until the end of the billing period, then automatically switch to the Free plan. No questions asked.

How many tables can I extract per day?+

There are no limits on the number of tables or pages. Extract as many as you need, as often as you need.

Is there a free trial for Pro?+

The Free plan is permanent and includes CSV/JSON export. You can try all the core functionality before deciding to upgrade to Pro for Excel, PDF and multi-tab features.
