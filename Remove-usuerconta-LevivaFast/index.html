Data safety & privacy

Delete your Leviva Fast account and data

You're in control of your information. This page explains, in plain terms, how to request deletion of your Leviva Fast account and what happens to your data afterward.

How to request it

01

Find the email you signed in with

Locate the Google email linked to your Leviva Fast account — that's how we identify which account to delete.

02

Cancel your subscription first

If you have an active Premium plan, cancel it in the Google Play Store under Payments & subscriptions. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel billing.

03

Send your request

Tap the button below to send a pre-filled email, or write directly to cadufelicioandrade@gmail.com.

04

Confirmation & timeline

You'll receive an email confirming receipt, and deletion is completed within 30 days, as detailed below.

What happens to your data

Permanently removed

  • Account profile (name, email, and photo linked to your sign-in)
  • Fasting history, completed windows and streaks
  • Preferences (fasting protocol, weight, gender, language, unit of measure)
  • Data synced to the cloud (Firestore)
  • Account authentication record

Kept when necessary

  • Aggregated, anonymous usage statistics that don't identify you
  • Ad identifiers already processed by partners (Google AdMob), unlinked from your account
  • Subscription and transaction records held by Google Play, which we do not control
  • Records required by law for a limited period (e.g. fraud prevention), where applicable
Retention period: once your request is confirmed, personal data is deleted within 30 days. Backup copies may take up to 90 days to be fully removed from our systems.
